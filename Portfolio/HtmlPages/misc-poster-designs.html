These posters are my early designs during my graphic design and illustration programs at Diablo Valley College. The final image on the right is the final graphic design course I have taken with the best poster design for a "how to".

These posters are my experiences being a designer for small local businesses around the East Bay. My design philosophy was to aim for playing off of their brand identity specifically with their colors and logos. Collaborations with the business plus with professors and my small team has significantly built up my skill and experience in visual marketing and design.
